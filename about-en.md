I am a **Senior Solution Architect** with more than 18 years of experience in banking systems development and in the adoption of DevOps, CI/CD and automation practices. My expertise spans a range of technologies, including **Java, C#, ASP.NET Core, Azure, and IBM i systems (RPGLE, SQLRPGLE, Full Free)**. I also have experience in mobile development with **Kotlin (Android) and Swift/Objective-C (iOS)**.

Beyond my work in software development, I am a **teacher and mentor** to new programmers, helping them strengthen their skills in Java, AS400 and DB2 databases. I'm passionate about teaching and about adopting good programming practices, modern standards and the responsible use of artificial intelligence in software development.

Within the **IBM Champions** program, I have been recognized as an **IBM Champion 2026**, which highlights my contribution to the developer community and my commitment to innovation in IBM technologies. In addition, within the **IBM Rising Champions** program, I have been recognized as **IBM Influencer 2025**, **IBM Advocate 2025** and **IBM Contributor 2025**, reflecting my commitment to innovation and to the developer community in IBM technologies.

Thanks to my support of the IBM i community, I have earned credentials within the **IBM i Community Program**, reaching the following levels on the community's badge path:
- **IBM i Community Contributor (Level 1)**
- **IBM i Community Influencer (Level 2)**
- **IBM i Community Advocate (Level 3)**

These achievements reflect a sustained and visible contribution to the IBM i community (active participation, technical leadership, content creation, and supporting and guiding other members), and they strengthen my professional standing in the IBM i / Power ecosystem.

- DevOps Engineer (CM-DOTP)

On this blog I share content about **DevOps, CI/CD, backend development, cloud architectures, programming best practices and analysis of AI tools**. My goal is to share knowledge and help the developer community improve their skills and optimize their development processes.
